Research Agenda

I am currently busy with research that is looking at the thermodynamic and kinetic modelling of low alloy dual phase and TRIP steels. The project work is a contribution to a broader project investigating more efficient methods for the design and optimization of materials using concepts from the framework of multidisciplinary design.

Research Interests: 

 - Thermodynamic modelling (Thermo-Calc) 

 - Kinetic modelling of heat treatments 

 - Neural Network classification of microstructural images

- Bayesian-based Design of materials
